Reference 2 — Teacher Summary Notes

Your teacher's official inquiry questions and discipline connections for Nominative Determinism

Science Inquiry Questions

  • Can a name directly affect biology, or does it influence behaviour through the environment?
  • How could repeated labels affect a person's behaviour over time?

I&S Inquiry Questions

  • Do labels and expectations shape identity?
  • Can society influence a person by attaching meaning to their name?
  • How might a name affect confidence, aspiration, or self-image?

Interdisciplinary Questions

  • Is identity partly shaped by what others expect from us?
  • Can a label become a self-fulfilling prophecy?
Teacher Summary

This resource explores how names may influence how a person is seen — and what they become. From a Science lens, repeated experiences, expectations, and surroundings can influence behaviour and self-perception over time. From an I&S lens, names carry culture, history, class, gender expectations, and social meaning. The resource supports exploring how identity develops through relationships, social interactions, and cultural influences — and raises the key IDU question: is identity shaped by what others expect from us?

Reference 03
Twin Study Meta-Analysis
Scientific data / bar chart · Polderman et al., Nature Genetics 2015
ScienceQuantitative DataPeer-Reviewed ★
Key Statistics
Studies analysed2,748
Twin pairs14.5 million
Traits measured17,804
Avg heritability~49%
Height MZ corr.0.92 (DZ: 0.47)
Most credible source in the unit. Largest twin study ever. MZ correlations consistently ~double DZ = statistical fingerprint of heritability. Published in Nature Genetics. Use specific numbers in all essays.

Key Information
Study designIdentical twins, 2-week diet
ResearcherProf. Tim Spector, KCL
